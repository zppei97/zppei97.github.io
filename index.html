Bio

 I grew uo in Jiaozuo, Henan, China. I recieved B.S. degree in computer science from WHU(Jun. 2019), where I am pursuing Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Wei Gong and Ge Han. My passions lie at the land-atmosphere interactions and inverse modeling. My goal is to realize independent evaluation of emission sources(especially for CO2 and CH4) using space-based observation. During my leisure time, I love playing badminton, swiming, and hiking.

裴志鹏，河南焦作人，武汉大学测绘遥感信息工程国家重点实验室2022级博士生，师从龚威教授和韩舸副教授，主要研究方向为遥感碳计量、城市尺度碳通量反演、甲烷排放源的卫星探测和量化。以第一/通讯作者在遥感/环境领域的顶级期刊(RSE、IEEE TGRS、STOEN)发表SCI论文5篇，总引用200余次，单篇一作最高被引100余次，2篇入选为ESI高被引论文，1篇入选ESI热点论文。曾担任 Optics Letters 期刊审稿人。曾获研究生一等学业奖学金、研究生国家奖学金。
